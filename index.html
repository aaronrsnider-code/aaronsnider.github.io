About Me

Yo! I'm Aaron, a 15 year old high school student from California. Ever since seventh grade, I've become addicted to streetwear, luxury fashion, and the culture surrounding it. I love to express myself through my outfits and share my passion for fashion with others. I hope to one day work in the fashion industry, whether it be as a designer, salesperson, looking over financials, or influencer. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new trends, attending fashion events, and connecting with like-minded individuals who share my love for style.

Experience

I attend Castro Valley High School, where I am a member of the Interact Club where I volunteer in community service events and fundraisers. Apart from that, I also went to NABA's ACAP summer camp (which you can read more about in the Experiences tab) where I networked to further my knowledge of the fashion industry and learned about the different career paths in fashion. I also have a Depop store where I sell streetwear and vintage clothing, which has allowed me to gain experience in e-commerce and marketing. Through these experiences, I have developed a strong work ethic, creativity, and a passion for fashion that I hope to continue to pursue in the future.
